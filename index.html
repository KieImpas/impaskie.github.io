Unveiling symbolism and themes in The Lantern in the Storm is exciting. It's like decoding a message of hope and resilience hidden in the narrative.

THE LANTERN IN THE STORM

Characters:

Eli – A brave young boy who loves the sea and helps others despite his fear.

The Villagers – People living in the coastal village who panic during the storm.

Sailors – Travelers at sea who depend on the lighthouse for safety.

SETTING:

The story takes place in a small coastal village near a lighthouse during a violent stormy night.

CONFLICT:
A powerful storm causes the lighthouse lamp to go out, putting ships and sailors in danger. Eli must overcome his fear to bring light back.

MORAL LESSON:
Even a small act of courage can make a big difference when others are in need.
